The single dial combination pad lock consists of two parts, a round shape dial and a bell-shaped hoop. On the back side, there is a key whole. The password can be reset by the key.





To open it, the system strongly relies on common knowledge. It is not a pedagogical object at all. There is no indication of which direction one should spin in order to open the lock.

Scroll

First, one has to reset the lock by spinning it clockwise at least 2 times pass 0.

Scroll

Then, stop at the first digit of the combination.

Scroll

Afterwards, spin it counterclockwise pass the first digit then stop at the second digit of the combination.

Scroll

Then one has to spin it clockwise again directly to the third digit.

Scroll

Last but not least, open the lock.
